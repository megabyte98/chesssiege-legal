Privacy Policy for Chess Siege

Effective date: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Megabyte Games ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Chess Siege mobile game (the "Game"). By using the Game, you acknowledge the practices described in this policy.

1. Information You Provide

Chess Siege does not currently require an account and does not ask you to provide your name, postal address, phone number, or payment-card details. If you contact us by email, we receive your email address and any information you include in your message.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The Game stores gameplay information locally on your device, including game progress, unlocked levels and cosmetic items, settings, virtual currency, lives, rewards, and purchase-entitlement status. This information is used to save your progress and provide Game features. You can remove local Game data through your device settings or by uninstalling the Game, subject to your device and platform backup settings.

3. Information Collected Automatically

The Game and its third-party service providers may automatically collect or process the following categories of information:

  • Device and app information: device model, manufacturer, operating-system version, language, app version, and general technical information.
  • Identifiers: app-instance or installation identifiers, advertising identifiers where available, and related device identifiers.
  • Network information: Internet Protocol (IP) address and approximate location that may be inferred from it.
  • Gameplay and usage information: level starts and results, tutorial progress, piece placement, feature interactions, session activity, ad interactions, and in-app purchase events.
  • Diagnostics: crash reports, stack traces, error messages, failed asset or service information, session identifiers, and device state associated with a crash or error.
  • Advertising information: ad requests, impressions, clicks, reward completion, and information used to deliver, measure, limit, and prevent fraud in advertising.
  • Purchase information: product identifiers, purchase state, and transaction or entitlement information supplied by Google Play. We do not receive your full payment-card details.

4. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

  • operate the Game and save or restore gameplay entitlements;
  • understand Game usage and improve gameplay, balance, and performance;
  • diagnose crashes, errors, and technical problems;
  • configure Game features remotely;
  • provide rewarded and interstitial advertisements;
  • process and restore in-app purchases;
  • measure advertising and prevent fraud or abuse;
  • respond to support requests and comply with legal obligations.

5. Advertising and Your Choices

Chess Siege uses Google AdMob to provide rewarded and interstitial ads. AdMob may use advertising identifiers, IP address, device information, and ad interaction data to deliver and measure ads. Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on applicable law, your region, your device or Google account settings, and the consent signals available to Google.

You can review or limit ad personalization through your Android device and Google account privacy settings. Where applicable law requires consent for advertising-related processing, an appropriate consent choice must be made available before that processing occurs. Withdrawing consent does not affect processing that occurred before withdrawal.

6. Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services for Android platform functionality. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob for advertising and ad measurement. How Google uses information from apps that use its services
  • Google Analytics for Firebase for gameplay and app-usage analytics. Firebase privacy and security information
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting and diagnostics. Firebase privacy and security information
  • Firebase Remote Config for remotely configuring Game features. Firebase privacy and security information
  • Google Play Billing for in-app purchases and purchase restoration. Google Payments Privacy Notice

These providers process information under their own privacy policies and may process information on servers located outside your country of residence.

7. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information for money. Information may be disclosed to the service providers listed above so they can provide analytics, diagnostics, advertising, configuration, billing, security, and platform services. Advertising-related disclosure may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" under some privacy laws even when no money is exchanged for the information.

Information may also be disclosed:

  • when required by law or valid legal process;
  • to protect users, our rights, or the security of the Game;
  • to investigate fraud, abuse, or violations of applicable terms;
  • as part of a merger, acquisition, financing, or transfer of the Game, subject to applicable legal requirements.

8. Legal Bases and International Processing

Where required by law, information is processed based on one or more of the following: performance of a service you request, our legitimate interests in operating and improving the Game, compliance with legal obligations, or your consent. Third-party providers may process information in other countries and apply their own legally recognized transfer safeguards.

9. Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device until it is deleted through the Game or device settings, cleared with the app's storage, or removed by uninstalling the Game, subject to platform backups. Email correspondence is kept only as long as reasonably necessary to provide support and meet legal obligations.

Analytics, advertising, crash, installation, and purchase information is retained according to the applicable Google service settings, contractual requirements, and Google retention policies. Retention periods may vary by data type and service.
